Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: 2025-08-23

Overview

DealRadarUS may earn commissions when you purchase products through links on our website. These affiliate partnerships help us keep our content free and support our deal research. You never pay extra when you buy through our affiliate links – the price remains the same, and we simply receive a small commission from the retailer.

Amazon Associates Program

As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases.

DealRadarUS is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com. When you click on Amazon product links and make purchases, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

How Affiliate Links Work & No Extra Cost

When you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, the retailer pays us a small commission. This commission is built into the retailer's marketing budget and does not increase the price you pay. Whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer's website, you'll pay the same price.

Our affiliate links help us:

  • Keep our website running and content free
  • Fund research to find the best deals
  • Maintain and improve our deal-finding tools

Editorial Independence

Our reviews, recommendations, and deal selections are not influenced by affiliate partnerships. We maintain complete editorial independence in choosing which products to feature and recommend. Our primary goal is to help you find genuine value and quality deals, regardless of commission potential.

We only recommend products and deals that we believe offer real value to our readers. If we wouldn't recommend it to a friend or family member, we won't recommend it to you.

How We Disclose Affiliate Relationships

We believe in complete transparency. Here's how we ensure you always know when content contains affiliate links:

Website Disclosures

  • Near Links: We place disclosures close to affiliate links, often using phrases like "(affiliate link)" or "(paid link)" right next to or immediately before the link
  • Article Level: Articles containing affiliate links include a disclosure near the top, clearly stating that the content contains affiliate links
  • Footer: Every page includes our affiliate disclosure in the footer

Mobile Considerations

We ensure our disclosures are easily visible on mobile devices by:

  • Placing key disclosures "above the fold" when reasonable
  • Using clear, readable text that doesn't blend into the background
  • Ensuring disclosures aren't hidden behind "read more" buttons when possible

Social Media

On social media platforms, we use clear disclosure hashtags and statements, such as:

  • https://www.facebook.com/DealRadarUSad or https://www.facebook.com/DealRadarUSaffiliate at the beginning of posts containing affiliate links
  • Clear statements like "(affiliate links)" in captions
  • Platform-specific disclosure tools when available, supplemented with additional clear language

Example social media disclosures:

  • "Great deals today! (affiliate links) https://www.facebook.com/DealRadarUSad"
  • "Our weekly deal roundup includes affiliate links https://www.facebook.com/DealRadarUSaffiliate"
