## Our Mission

DealRadarUS helps readers understand fast-moving crypto markets with clear reporting, practical guides, and evidence-based analysis.

## What We Cover

- Daily crypto news and market-moving updates
- Deep analysis on Bitcoin, Ethereum, and major altcoin trends
- Step-by-step guides for new and experienced users
- Exchange and product reviews focused on real usability

## Editorial Principles

- Clarity over hype
- Risk awareness in every article
- Useful context, not just headlines
- Transparent affiliate disclosure when links are monetized

## Who We Serve

We publish for global readers, from first-time crypto users to active traders and long-term investors.
