## Step 2: Create and Verify Your Account

Due to global regulations, you must complete **KYC (Know Your Customer)** verification.
1. Sign up using your email or phone number.
2. Upload a photo of your government-issued ID (Passport or Driver's License).
3. Take a selfie for liveness detection.
*Verification typically takes less than 5 minutes.*

## Step 3: Deposit Funds

You can fund your account using various methods:
- **Bank Transfer (ACH/SEPA)**: Lowest fees, but may take 1-3 days.
- **Credit/Debit Card**: Instant, but fees can be high (1.5% - 3%).
- **P2P Trading**: Buy directly from other users (popular in regions with banking restrictions).

## Step 4: Place Your Buy Order

Once your funds arrive:
1. Navigate to the **Spot Market** or "Buy Crypto" section.
2. Select **Bitcoin (BTC)**.
3. Choose your order type:
   - **Market Order**: Buy immediately at the current price. (Recommended for beginners)
   - **Limit Order**: Set a specific price you want to pay.
4. Enter the amount and click **Buy BTC**.

## Step 5: Secure Your Bitcoin

**"Not your keys, not your coins."**
If you plan to hold more than $1,000 worth of crypto, move it off the exchange into a personal wallet.
- **Hardware Wallet**: Ledger or Trezor (Most secure).
- **Software Wallet**: Trust Wallet or MetaMask.

## Conclusion

Congratulations! You are now a Bitcoin owner. Remember, investing is a marathon, not a sprint. Consider **Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA)**—buying a small amount regularly—to smooth out volatility.
