## The Cycle of Wealth Rotation

In every crypto cycle, wealth rotates in a predictable pattern:
1. **Bitcoin** leads the rally (First mover).
2. **Ethereum** catches up.
3. **Large Caps** (Solana, BNB, etc.) pump.
4. **Mid/Small Caps** go parabolic (Altseason).

Currently, we are in the transition from Phase 1 to Phase 2. Bitcoin Dominance (BTC.D) is hovering around 54%, facing stiff resistance.

## Bitcoin Dominance (BTC.D) Analysis

BTC.D has been in an uptrend for 2 years. However, the chart is showing signs of exhaustion:
- **Bearish Divergence** on the weekly RSI.
- **Double Top** formation at 55%.

A breakdown below 52% would confirm the start of Altseason.
